Dream Perfect Regime

Dream perfect regime (dpr) is a multigenre music and video group. We create, direct, produce,
and edit all types of visual work as well as curate artists that derive from various musical
backgrounds and influences. Based in Seoul, our collective's main focus is to engage all
audiences by actualizing a distinctive audiovisual experience.
